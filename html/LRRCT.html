About Us

Late Ranjanben Ramanlal Charitable Trust was first started in 2016. The idea behind building this trust was to serve people, protect our environment and spread happiness. This idea was first initiated by our President Mr. Mukesh Patel, because of his mother (Ranjanben) and father (Ramanlal), as they used to help other people and provide them with food and help students with their financial aid and carry out other utilities as well so this inspired him to start this trust and also because of that, he named this trust after his parents. Our trust laid down many activities, such as kanyadaan, paying fees for underprivileged students, entertainment for Senior citizens, we also provided food and beverages to needy people. Our trust also planted trees and plants and took out a rally to create awareness among people to lead a healthy and safe lifestyle in our environment.

Our trust also helps out physically and mentally challenged people, we provide them with financial aid, medical aid and entertain them by taking them on trips and playing sports with them. After 2016, our trust was slowed down a bit, but in 2019, our trust paced up and started doing all the major activities again and we aim to create a healthy society by 2025.

Our Goal

Our main goal is to create a village for senior citizens, which will have all the utilities and facilities, such as houses, markets, hospitals, and many other amenities. We wish to create a healthy and safe society for all senior citizens.

ACTIVITIES

  • Entertainment programs for children, disabled people, and senior citizens.
  • Cooking program for the 30 women who had a disability of vision.

  • Carried out various roadshows in societies and on roads to create awareness among people regarding Covid- 19.
